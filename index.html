Taxi Tab is an app that will make your life easier when trying to split the bill. Once this app is completed, there will be no more fumbling about with calculators trying to see who is trying to get out of paying less for your ride.

All of the information displayed in this app isn't really all that difficult to find. The convenience is it's all in
one location.

As always, you should have MORE fare than required. This is just common curtesy, and you should always be prepared (but not obligated) to tip your driver.

Solely authored by Eric Cross.
